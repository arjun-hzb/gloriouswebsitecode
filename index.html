About Our School

Since 1999

25+ Years of Academic Excellence

Established in 1999, Glorious Public School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Hazaribagh for over two decades. We offer a comprehensive learning journey from Nursery to Class 10th, ensuring a strong foundation for every student.

Our campus features Smart Classrooms for digital learning and offers separate hostel facilities for Boys and Girls, providing a safe and disciplined residential experience.
