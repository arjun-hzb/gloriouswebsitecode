  • हर दिन बच्चों की पढ़ाई का वीडियो सीधे माता-पिता एवं Relatives के WhatsApp पर भेजने की बेमिसाल सुविधा।
  • प्रत्येक बच्चे का स्कूल के YouTube, Instagram and Facebook पर धमाकेदार उपस्थिति से अभिभावक बेहद उत्साहित हैं।
  • Class-I से ही बच्चों को Competitive Exams, Interviews Entrance की तैयारी कराई जाती.

Principal's Message

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Welcome to Glorious Public School, Maheshra, Hazaribagh. As the Principal, it is a privilege to lead an institution that has spent over 25 years fostering academic excellence, integrity, and a lifelong passion for learning. In today's rapidly changing global landscape, our mission goes beyond textbook education. We aim to nurture young individuals into responsible, innovative, and empathetic citizens equipped to face future challenges with utmost confidence.

At Glorious Public School, we strive to offer an inclusive learning ecosystem where modern pedagogical methods seamlessly integrate with deep values. Our state-of-the-art smart classrooms, fully equipped laboratories, comprehensive dynamic sports curricula, and premium separate secure residential hostel facilities for both boys and girls ensure a truly comprehensive and enriching educational journey.
