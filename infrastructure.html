Campus Infrastructure

Providing a world-class environment for the leaders of tomorrow.

Smart Classrooms

Our academic blocks are equipped with digitized learning tools. High-speed internet connectivity and interactive smart boards ensure that our students engage with a curriculum that is both visually stimulating and technologically advanced.

Modern Laboratories

To foster a spirit of inquiry, GPS Maheshra provides specialized labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Our facilities are designed to meet international safety standards while providing the best equipment for practical research.

Resource Center

The library serves as the intellectual heart of our school. With an extensive collection of over 5,000 volumes, academic journals, and a dedicated digital e-learning corner, we provide a peaceful environment for deep study.

Safe Commute

Student safety is our highest priority. Our transport fleet is managed by experienced drivers and monitored via GPS tracking. Every bus follows strict security protocols to ensure a safe journey across Hazaribagh.
